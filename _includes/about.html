Journey

A glance over some beautiful years of my life ....

  • 2016-2020

    B.Tech., IIT Delhi

    In India, more than 10 Lakhs people appear for the IIT entrance examination dreaming to get admission to IITs. I appeared for JEE-Advanced, India's toughest exam, and secured a 1070 rank that got me into one of the most premium institutes in India, IIT Delhi. I graduated from IIT Delhi in the year 2020.

  • 2019-2020

    Internships

    Being a student at IIT Delhi, I had gained lots of skills. But what was lacking was the industry experience. Hence, to apply my learnings to industry-level projects, I did multiples internships in the field of data science.

  • 2020

    Open-Source Contribution

    In the last semester of my B.Tech. at IIT Delhi, I got an opportunity to work with DataKind, Bangalore Chapter, as a data scientist. At DataKind, I worked on projects that aimed to develop an open-source data platform where researchers can come and query for what they need for their research.

  • 2020-Present

    Started my professional journey with JLR

    During the campus placements at IIT Delhi, I received an offer from Jaguar Land Rover for the Data Science Role. I joined Jaguar Land Rover in September 2020 and have worked on some cutting edge technologies since I joined the company
